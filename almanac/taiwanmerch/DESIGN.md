# Taiwan Merch — SP2 Video Visual Identity

## Style Prompt

Modern Taiwan with affection. Taipei night-market neon meets mountain stillness. Cyan neon accents on deep night canvas. Confident percussive motion (synthwave 100bpm pacing). Bold condensed sans display headlines carry the street-sign DNA; clean bold sans body keeps captions readable on video. Energetic but never touristy. This is a brand for someone who's been to Taiwan or is about to go.

## Colors

| Hex       | Role                                                                |
| --------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| `#06b6d4` | Primary — cyan (Taipei neon, key-word emphasis, brand mark)         |
| `#0d0e14` | Near-black blue — primary canvas, deep visual ground                |
| `#1a1d2e` | Off-night blue — secondary surface                                  |
| `#fafafa` | Off-white — primary caption text on dark scrim                      |
| `#a3a3a3` | Mid grey — secondary text                                           |
| `#e879f9` | Magenta — accent on lantern/temple/Taipei-101 reveal moments (rare) |

Caption-on-image style: white body text + cyan accent words in italic display. All caption text sits on a dark blue gradient scrim so it reads cleanly over varied night-market b-roll.

## Typography

- **Display headlines** (60-130px): `Oswald` weight 500-700 — condensed sans, Taipei street-sign DNA
- **Accent emphasis words**: `Oswald` weight 600 italic + uppercase, cyan
- **Captions / body** (38-58px): `Inter` weight 700 — bold sans for video legibility
- **Lower-third labels** (20-28px): `Inter` weight 500, letter-spacing 0.20em uppercase, cyan

## Motion

- Confident percussive (0.5-1.0s entrance durations — slightly faster than UtamaSpice, slightly slower than CC)
- Easing: `power3.out` for headline drops; `expo.out` for revelation moments; `power2.inOut` for ambient transitions
- Staggers: 50-100ms between elements (synthwave energy)
- Image Ken Burns: 1.0 → 1.08 scale over scene duration
- Scene transitions: crossfade with beat-snap (synthwave drums)

## What NOT to do

- No orientalism cliches: "exotic", "mysterious East", "land of contrasts" — Taiwan is a specific place, not a vibe
- No "Asian fusion" or generic East-Asian framing — Taiwanese has its own DNA
- Always name the place. Taipei, Kaohsiung, Tainan, Hualien, Jiufen — never just "Taiwan" as abstract
- Always name the food, market, temple if invoked. Shilin xiaolongbao not "street food"; Longshan not "a temple"
- No Mandarin pinyin in captions unless it's a place/brand name (Jiufen, Taipei 101, Din Tai Fung). English captions only.
- No rainbow LED gradients on dark backgrounds (H.264 banding ugly)
- No more than 2 font families per composition
- **No em-dashes in any caption or title text** — replace with periods, commas, colons (Jon-wide brand voice rule)
- Never reference the brand or products in beats 1-6; outro card only.
